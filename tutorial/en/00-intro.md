# Tutorial: AIAvatar — a digital twin in Telegram

A step-by-step guide to building a Telegram bot with personality, memory, and a content engine.

## What you'll get

A bot that:
- Converses like a real person — with its own character, opinions, and mood
- Remembers people and relationships with them — from friends to foes
- Posts content on a schedule — news, jokes, quizzes, wellness reminders
- Intervenes in conflicts and adjusts its tone to match the conversation partner
- Works in groups and private chats, with different personas for different chats
